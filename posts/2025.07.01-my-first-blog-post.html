My First Blog Post

Why do I even care?

July 1, 2025 · 1 min read

This is my first blog post. I love writing. I love love love writing. But what I have written, my entire body of work, is next to nothing. So, it would appear that although I love writing, I do not write. But it will have to change. I should write more. And that is why I came up with the idea of this blog.

But then, there is also this matter of... how much to let on? I am a private person, and the possibility of any human reading this post is next to nil. So, an element of anonymity is always there. But, even while being anonymous, do I want to share the thoughts I have? I am not so sure. The deepest thoughts probably should remain unsaid. I do not remember who, but someone said something like, when something comes out of the mouth (or pen or keyboard) it is already sullied. It would mean that there is a school of thought which believes that the unsaid is the most pristine something could ever be.

Anyway. Here I am. And here I write.
